The six default sections of a Debrief brief are Why / People / Timeline / Next Steps / Risk Flags / Promises. Admins can rename them, reorder them, or remove sections that do not fit the team's vocabulary.

## Where the settings live

In your Dunamis Studios dashboard, navigate to the entitlement for the portal you want to configure:

1. Sign in at dunamisstudios.net.
2. Open the Debrief entitlement for the target portal.
3. Click **Brief sections**.

You see the current section list, labels, and order.

## Renaming a section

Click the label to edit in place. Labels must be 1 to 40 characters. The change applies to every brief generated after save. Existing Notes attached to past records are not rewritten.

## Reordering

Drag to reorder. The order in the config is the order in the generated brief.

## Removing a section

Click the minus icon next to a section. You can remove up to three of the six. At least three must remain so the brief has enough structure to be useful.

If a removed section had non-empty content in a past brief, that content stays in the historical Note. Removal only affects briefs generated after save.

## Adding a section

Admin-added sections live below the defaults. They are labeled "Custom" in the scope editor and can be renamed. You provide a brief description that tells the AI what to put in that section.

## Per-handoff-type overrides

Section configurations are global to the portal by default. To override per handoff type (so BDR → AE uses one label set and AE → CS uses another), see [Per-handoff-type context overrides](/help/debrief-admin/per-handoff-context-overrides).
