Dunamis Studios is the company behind Debrief (handoff intelligence for HubSpot CRM) and Property Pulse (real-time deal health signal for HubSpot CRM). Both are HubSpot marketplace apps.

## The account model

One Dunamis Studios account covers every app and every portal.

- You sign up once at dunamisstudios.net.
- That account holds entitlements for every HubSpot portal where you install our apps.
- If you run Debrief on three portals and Property Pulse on two, that is five entitlements on one account.
- Billing is per-portal-per-product (see [Per-portal pricing, and why](/help/billing/per-portal-pricing)).

Sign in once, and the dashboard shows every portal's subscription status, credit balance, and billing history in one place.

## What we build

HubSpot marketplace apps. Narrow, opinionated, useful.

- **Debrief.** Generates a structured brief and handoff message when a CRM record changes owners.
- **Property Pulse.** Tracks deal health signals across HubSpot in real time.

We do not do consulting. We do not build custom integrations. We do not resell HubSpot. What we ship is on the marketplace or nowhere.

## What we do not do

- We do not aggregate CRM data across customers. Every portal's data is processed in isolation. Nothing crosses between customers.
- We do not train models on customer data. The AI in Debrief uses your portal's context to generate one brief, and that context is not retained for training.
- We do not sell your data. We are a SaaS company paid by subscription. That is the whole business model.
